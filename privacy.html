Privacy Policy

FitSutra — Track · Reduce · Maintain

Last updated: 8 May 2026

VKS Tech ("we", "us", "our") operates the FitSutra mobile and web application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

In short: We only collect what's needed to run your fitness tracking. We don't sell your data, show ads, or share information with third parties beyond essential services (Google Firebase for storage, Gemini AI for nutrition lookups).

1. Information We Collect

FitSutra collects only what's necessary to provide fitness tracking:

  • Account information: Phone number (for OTP login) or email address (for Google sign-in).
  • Profile information: Name, age, gender, height, weight, and fitness goals — provided by you during onboarding.
  • Health and fitness data: Food logs, water intake, exercise sessions, weight history, body measurements, step counts.
  • Approximate location: Used only to fetch local weather for climate-based hydration goals. We do not continuously track your location or store location history.
  • Device information: Browser type, device type, and time zone — collected automatically for app functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to:

  • Track your fitness progress and provide personalized insights
  • Calculate calorie, water, and nutrient targets based on your profile
  • Send water reminder notifications (only with your permission)
  • Adjust hydration recommendations based on local temperature
  • Provide AI-powered nutrition advice (food queries are anonymized)
  • Sync your data across your devices via your account

3. How We Store and Protect Your Data

Your data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers (Google Cloud Platform). All data is:

  • Encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Encrypted at rest by Firebase
  • Protected by Firebase Authentication
  • Accessible only to you via your authenticated account

4. Third-Party Services

FitSutra uses these third-party services to provide functionality:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore Database) — for user accounts and data storage. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini AI — for nutrition lookups when foods aren't in our database. Only food names are sent (no personal data). Gemini Terms
  • wttr.in — free weather service for climate-based hydration. Receives only approximate coordinates (city-level), no personal data.

We do not use any analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.) or advertising networks.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties, except:

  • When required by law (e.g., court order, legal process)
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of FitSutra, our users, or the public
  • To service providers strictly necessary for app functionality (listed in Section 4)

6. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app at any time.
  • Export: Contact us to receive a copy of your data.
  • Delete: You can delete your account at any time via the in-app Profile settings, or by visiting our account deletion page.
  • Correct: Update your profile information at any time within the app.
  • Withdraw consent: Disable notifications, location access, or account features through device settings.

7. Children's Privacy

FitSutra is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us at the email below for immediate deletion.

8. Account Deletion

To delete your FitSutra account and all associated data:

  • In-app: Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account
  • Web: Visit fitsutra.vkstech.com/delete-account.html
  • Email: Send a deletion request to our support email

Account deletion is permanent and removes all your data within 30 days.

9. Cookies and Local Storage

FitSutra uses local storage (similar to cookies) on your device to:

  • Keep you logged in between sessions
  • Cache your preferences for offline use
  • Store your fitness data locally as a backup

We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through the app or via email. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, your data, or how to exercise your rights, please contact us:
