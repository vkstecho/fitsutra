FitSutra · Account & Data Deletion Request

⚠️ Warning: Account deletion is permanent. All your fitness logs, weight history, food logs, water tracking, exercise sessions, and profile data will be removed. This action cannot be undone.

How to Delete Your FitSutra Account

You can delete your account using any of the methods below. We respect your right to control your data and provide multiple ways to remove it from our systems.

Method 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

  1. Open the FitSutra app on your phone
  2. Tap the Profile icon at the bottom right
  3. Tap the Settings tab
  4. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm by entering your phone number or email
  6. Your account and all data will be deleted immediately

Open FitSutra

Method 2: Email Request

Send an email to our support team requesting account deletion. Include:

  • Your registered phone number or email
  • Subject line: "Delete My FitSutra Account"
  • Confirmation that you want all data permanently deleted

📧 Send Deletion Email

We will process your request within 30 days and send a confirmation email once complete.

What Data May Be Retained

Per legal and security requirements, some data may be retained for a limited period:

  • Anonymized usage logs: Stripped of all personal identifiers, used for app improvement (retained up to 90 days)
  • Legal compliance records: If required by law (e.g., for fraud prevention or legal requests)

This retained data cannot be linked back to you personally.

Timeline for Deletion

  • In-app deletion: Immediate (within seconds)
  • Email request: Processed within 7 business days
  • Backup purge: Backups containing your data are purged within 30 days
  • Confirmation: You'll receive an email confirmation once deletion is complete

Re-creating Your Account

After deletion, you can sign up again at any time using the same phone number or email. However, your previous data will not be recoverable — you'll start fresh.
